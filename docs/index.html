Data sources & dates

This map combines several public datasets of different vintages. Each street's values carry a source tag in its info panel. Source detail and field definitions are in the CODEBOOK.

Street network & sidewalksOpenStreetMap — retrieved June 2026 (live snapshot)
Speed limits, lanes, width, medianCity of Houston Public Works (Traffic_gx) — retrieved June 2026
Traffic volume (ADT) & operating speedCity of Houston Public Works count stations — readings span 2012–2026; most recent per location used
Adjacent land useHarris County Appraisal District, via City of Houston — retrieved June 2026
Neighborhood demographicsU.S. Census ACS 2023 5-year estimates (covering 2019–2023)
Crashes (per-street counts)TxDOT CRIS public extracts — 2016–2024 + partial 2026 (2020 & 2025 pending); each crash assigned to its nearest street within 200 ft
District boundaryCity of Houston GIS — current council districts (post-2020 redistricting)

"No data" means a value isn't recorded in the source — not that the feature is necessarily absent on the ground (this matters most for sidewalks and posted speeds, which the city records mainly on larger streets). Where the city hasn't posted a speed limit, Texas law sets a default of 30 mph, shown as "TX default".
